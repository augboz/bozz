Windows may flag the installer as unsafe or say it “could harm your device”. It’s a false alarm: Bozz is open-source and not yet code-signed (a certificate is on the way). If your browser blocks the download, the simplest fix is to try a different browser: Firefox usually lets it through. Otherwise, in Edge or Chrome open your downloads, choose Keep, then Keep anyway, and on the blue “Windows protected your PC” screen click More info, then Run anyway.

macOS isn't notarized yet. If macOS says “Bozz is damaged and can’t be opened”, drag Bozz into Applications, then paste this into Terminal and press Return:

xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/Bozz.app
