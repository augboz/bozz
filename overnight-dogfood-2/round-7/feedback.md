# Round 7 — board meeting (final round)

Board average satisfaction: **6/10** (plateaued at 6; the board keeps raising the
bar rather than signing off). Still wants more.

## The board's reframe
"Bozz has won the SEEING half of its north star and punted the DOING half. It is
all intake and zero doing — a beautiful dashboard you glance at, not one you run
your day from." Their proposed agenda:
1. **Auto-Plan My Day** — fill free calendar gaps with timed task blocks.
2. **Effort-aware deadlines** (S/M/L) + a "start now" warning.
3. **Start-my-day execution + end-of-day close-out + clear-streak.**
4. **Snooze/defer** on every row.

## Strategic decision (why we did NOT build P1 this round)
P1 "Auto-Plan My Day" is an auto-scheduler. The Round 6 CEO — citing the LOCKED
founder-offsite direction — explicitly vetoed AI/auto-scheduling as the exact
anti-reference (Sunsama/Akiflow) Bozz will not become, and that matches the
recorded product direction. Each board meeting spawns fresh members with no memory
of that veto, so this round's board re-proposed it. Building a headline feature
that contradicts the owner's locked strategy autonomously (overnight) is the wrong
call. **The auto-planner is flagged as a decision for the founder**, not shipped.

## What WAS built (the safe, on-strategy "doing" loop)
- **Snooze / defer** on every priority + deadline row (Tomorrow / This weekend /
  Next week) — the triage connective tissue the app lacked entirely.
- **End-of-day close-out + daily clear-streak** — a celebratory "today's done —
  N cleared" moment + a streak (persisted like Habits) + one-tap "roll unfinished
  to tomorrow". The retention reward loop.
- **Effort-aware deadlines** (optional S/M/L) — sort the Deadlines hub by effort
  vs time-left, and an INFORMATIONAL "start now" warning (no auto-scheduling).

(Two A/B experiment agents hit retry caps in earlier rounds; board verdicts were
unaffected.)

This is the final planned round. The board is an intentionally aggressive panel
that will keep proposing the next frontier; satisfaction has plateaued at 6 not
because the work is weak but because the bar keeps rising (now to a full DOING/
auto-planner suite). The product is at a high, coherent, on-strategy plateau. The
remaining ambitions (the auto-planner fork, the student Grades tracker) are
documented in FINAL-REPORT.md as decisions for the founder.
