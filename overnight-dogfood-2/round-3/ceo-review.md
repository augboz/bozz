with zero topics. Plus a day-2 coach chip gives the returning user a "what now".

brief. The loop the north star promises is now actually closed for a new student.

The activation + daily loop are strong now, but the all-term stickiness bets the
board has repeatedly flagged remain: a glanceable weekly Timetable widget on the
home grid, and the Grades/GPA tracker. There's also a fuller "good morning" ritual
(I shipped the lighter coach chip first, on purpose, as the lower-risk version).

Decision: CONTINUE. Round 4: a weekly Timetable widget + the Grades/GPA tracker.
