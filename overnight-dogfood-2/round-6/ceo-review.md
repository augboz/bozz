## Why it moved
This round both deepened the product AND corrected its aim. The week is now a
first-class glance (Briefing | Week | Board), the keyboard runs the product
(Ctrl+K does things, not just jumps), and the brief reaches the user proactively
via free local nudges. Just as important: the board pulled the loop back onto the
locked strategy (22-38 knowledge worker; no AI auto-scheduling; no freelancer
second-product), which is healthier than chasing every persona.

## Where we are after 6 rounds
The core promise is delivered end to end and on-strategy: onboard (type your
timetable / cold-start) → land on a Briefing or Week → see your day/week in
seconds with a live next-event banner → capture by typing or Ctrl+K → everything
that's due lives in one Deadlines hub → get nudged before it's late. No blank
canvas, no hidden engine, no off-brand sprawl.
