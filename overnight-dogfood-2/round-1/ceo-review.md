# Round 1 — CEO / board review

- Board satisfaction with the PRE-round state: **5/10** (board wants more rounds).
- Self-assessed comfort AFTER this round's changes: **~6.5/10**.

## Why it moved
The single biggest gap the board named — the home not delivering the morning
promise — is now materially better: Today auto-aggregates real deadline/event
signals instead of only the manual plan, deadlines show live urgency, and triage
is one tap. These are the foundations the rest of the plan builds on.

## Why not higher / what's next
The board's headline bets are still ahead: a cold-start that builds the dashboard
FOR the student (so the brief has real data on minute one), the recurring
timetable (the #1 student need), and the Grades/GPA stickiness hook. Until a new
user lands on populated, personal data, the brief still shows the empty fallback.

Decision: CONTINUE. Round 2 takes the user-preferred experiment: a conversational/
templated cold-start that seeds real topics + sample deadlines.
