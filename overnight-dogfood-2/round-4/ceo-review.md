# Round 4 — CEO / board review

- Board satisfaction with the pre-round state: **5/10** (a reframe dip — the board
  realised the brief shipped as a "kit"; wanted more).
- Self-assessed comfort AFTER this round: **~8.3/10**.

## Why it jumped
This round delivered the felt payoff, not just the plumbing. The morning brief is
now the DEFAULT landing for a new student (full-width, zero-config) instead of a
card to discover and assemble. Typed quick-add finally uses the offline parser, so
"essay due friday for bio" becomes a dated, topic-routed task in one keystroke and
shows up in the brief. And the timetable is first-class: recurring classes with a
room and a live "Next class in 18 min" banner. Crucially, returning users are
untouched (verified: existing account still loads its Board layout).

## Why not 9+/next
The all-term stickiness hook the board has flagged for several rounds — a Grades/
GPA tracker — is still not built, and that's the emotional reason a student keeps
the app open across a term. A fuller active "good morning" ritual is also still a
lighter version than the board's ideal.

Decision: CONTINUE. Round 5: the Grades & GPA tracker (per-module assessments,
weighted average, target/"what do I need" calculator).
